📖 About Clinic & Court

Welcome to Clinic & Court, a space where medicine meets motion, and rhythm flows through recovery. Founded by a med student with a passion for neurophysiology, lyrical storytelling, and athletic performance, this blog blends clinical insight with cultural energy. From analyzing tendon loading to exploring music’s healing power, every post aims to educate, inspire, and connect minds that move. Karibu sana—this is a journey of systems, soul, and the court beneath your feet.
